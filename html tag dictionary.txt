a - creates a hyperlink to websites
	attribute: href
	ex. <a href = "http://www.youtube.com"> Youtube!</a>

h1 - the biggest header, puts text on another line
	attribute: none
	ex. <h1> hi </h1>

h2 - similar to h1 but the text is a bit smaller
	attribute: none
	ex. <h2> hi <h2>

img - puts in an image into the document
	attribute: src
	ex. <img src ="csgo.jpg">
	
li - it is used to add on to the list, creates a new list item
	attribute: none
	<li> food </li>
	
ol - it creates a list within it, uses numbers
	attribute: none
	ex. 
	<ol>
		<li>Toxic teamates</li>
		<li>Dying early in game</li>
		<li>eating too much</li>
	</ol>
	
p - represents a paragraph within the space between the two tags
	attribute: none
	example: 
	<p>I am Ethan Fang and I go to Lord Byng Secondary, and I am currently in grade 9. My faviorte classes include comp-sci and social studies. Some of my hobbies include gaming and chess.</p>

ul - similar to ol tag, creates a list but uses bullet points
	attribute: none
	example:
	<ul>
		<li>CS:GO</li>
		<li>food</li>
		<li>PubG</li>
	</ul>
